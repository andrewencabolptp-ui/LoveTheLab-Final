Lab the Love is a capstone project developed by Grade 12 Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics Strand Section Quisumbing of Francisco P. Tolentino Integrated High School School Year 2024-2025 to address the challenges students face in understanding and using laboratory equipment safely and effectively. As an offline learning app, it serves as an accessible and user-friendly educational companion that provides clear instructions on equipment functions, safety protocols, and laboratory best practices.

Tailored for Science, TLE, and Computer laboratories, Lab the Love helps students build confidence in handling lab tools while fostering a culture of curiosity, responsibility, and innovation. Whether you're a beginner learning the basics or an aspiring scientist preparing for advanced experiments, our app ensures you approach every lab activity with knowledge and care.

With Lab the Love, students can:

✅ Explore a detailed catalog of lab equipment
✅ Understand safety guidelines to prevent accidents
✅ Develop practical skills for real-world applications

This project is developed in partial fulfillment of the requirements for our Capstone, under the guidance of our adviser, Mdme. Arrah Khay Casidsid-Soliman. Our goal is to bridge the knowledge gap and provide a reliable educational tool that supports students, teachers, and schools in creating a safer and smarter learning environment.

Join us in loving the lab!
